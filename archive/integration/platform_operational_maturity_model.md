# Colaberry Sentinel OS — Platform Operational Maturity Model

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the official platform operational maturity model governing long-term operational evolution, governance-aware organizational scaling, replay-safe maturity progression, deterministic capability advancement, institutional resilience growth, and explainable enterprise operational excellence across Sentinel OS.

The purpose of this maturity model is to ensure:

* Structured operational capability growth
* Deterministic organizational evolution
* Replay-safe institutional progression
* Governance-visible maturity advancement
* Explainable operational scaling
* Environment-safe enterprise evolution
* Sustainable long-term platform resilience

Operational maturity SHALL prioritize institutional integrity, replayability, governance visibility, ethical continuity, and survivability over uncontrolled expansion or short-term optimization.

---

# 2. Operational Maturity Philosophy

## Core Principles

Operational maturity SHALL:

* Preserve operational visibility
* Preserve replayability
* Preserve governance visibility
* Preserve institutional continuity
* Preserve accountability lineage
* Prevent hidden operational mutation
* Support deterministic organizational growth
