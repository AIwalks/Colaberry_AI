# Colaberry Sentinel OS — Enterprise Platform Closure and Transition Governance

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the official enterprise platform closure and transition governance framework governing platform retirement, operational succession, governance-safe transition workflows, replay-safe institutional handoff, deterministic organizational continuity, and explainable enterprise decommissioning across Sentinel OS.

The purpose of this framework is to ensure:

* Controlled platform transition governance
* Deterministic organizational handoff
* Replay-safe institutional continuity
* Governance-visible platform retirement
* Explainable operational succession
* Environment-safe decommissioning execution
* Sustainable long-term institutional preservation

Platform closure and transition governance SHALL prioritize institutional truthfulness, replayability, governance visibility, ethical continuity, and survivability over operational convenience or accelerated retirement.

---

# 2. Platform Closure Philosophy

## Core Principles

Platform closure governance SHALL:

* Preserve operational visibility
* Preserve replayability
* Preserve governance visibility
* Preserve institutional continuity
* Preserve accountability lineage
* Prevent hidden transition mutation
* Support deterministic organizational succession
