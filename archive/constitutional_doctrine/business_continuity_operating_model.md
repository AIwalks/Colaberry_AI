# Colaberry Sentinel OS — Business Continuity Operating Model

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the official business continuity operating model governing enterprise survivability, operational continuity coordination, governance-aware disruption management, replay-safe continuity workflows, deterministic organizational recovery, and explainable continuity orchestration across Sentinel OS.

The purpose of this operating model is to ensure:

* Continuous governed operations during disruption
* Deterministic continuity coordination
* Replay-safe organizational recovery
* Governance-visible continuity management
* Explainable operational stabilization
* Environment-safe continuity execution
* Sustainable enterprise resilience

Business continuity SHALL prioritize operational truthfulness, replayability, governance visibility, ethical stability, and containment safety over convenience or hidden continuity automation.

---

# 2. Business Continuity Philosophy

## Core Principles

Business continuity SHALL:

* Preserve operational visibility
* Preserve replayability
* Preserve governance visibility
* Preserve lineage continuity
* Preserve organizational accountability
* Prevent hidden continuity mutation
* Support deterministic restoration
