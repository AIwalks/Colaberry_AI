# Colaberry Sentinel OS — Disaster Recovery Governance Framework

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the official disaster recovery governance framework governing catastrophic failure recovery, governance-aware restoration orchestration, replay-safe continuity recovery, deterministic infrastructure restoration, operational survivability coordination, and explainable systemic recovery across Sentinel OS.

The purpose of this framework is to ensure:

* Deterministic disaster recovery execution
* Replay-safe operational restoration
* Governance-visible catastrophic recovery
* Controlled containment escalation
* Explainable restoration sequencing
* Environment-safe infrastructure recovery
* Sustainable enterprise survivability

Disaster recovery SHALL prioritize operational truthfulness, replayability, governance visibility, containment safety, and forensic continuity over recovery speed alone.

---

# 2. Disaster Recovery Philosophy

## Core Principles

Disaster recovery SHALL:

* Preserve operational visibility
* Preserve replayability
* Preserve governance visibility
* Preserve lineage continuity
* Preserve environment isolation
* Prevent hidden restoration mutation
* Support deterministic recovery
