# Colaberry Sentinel OS — Constitutional Resilience and Survivability Model

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the official constitutional resilience and survivability model governing foundational system integrity, existential operational continuity, governance-safe constitutional recovery, replay-safe platform survivability, deterministic institutional restoration, and explainable systemic endurance across Sentinel OS.

The purpose of this model is to ensure:

* Foundational operational survivability
* Deterministic constitutional recovery
* Replay-safe institutional continuity
* Governance-visible existential stabilization
* Explainable systemic endurance
* Environment-safe constitutional restoration
* Sustainable long-term organizational resilience

Constitutional resilience SHALL prioritize operational truthfulness, replayability, governance visibility, ethical continuity, and institutional survivability over convenience or short-term operational optimization.
