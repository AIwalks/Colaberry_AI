# Colaberry Sentinel OS — Institutional Knowledge Preservation and Legacy Governance

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the official institutional knowledge preservation and legacy governance framework governing long-term knowledge continuity, governance-safe historical preservation, replay-safe organizational memory, deterministic institutional succession, and explainable enterprise legacy stewardship across Sentinel OS.

The purpose of this framework is to ensure:

* Institutional knowledge survivability
* Deterministic organizational memory preservation
* Replay-safe historical continuity
* Governance-visible legacy stewardship
* Explainable institutional succession
* Environment-safe archival governance
* Sustainable long-term enterprise continuity

Institutional knowledge preservation SHALL prioritize historical truthfulness, replayability, governance visibility, ethical continuity, and organizational survivability over convenience or short-term operational optimization.
