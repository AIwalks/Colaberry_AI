# Colaberry Sentinel OS — Meta Operating Contract

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the highest-level operating contract governing Sentinel OS behavior, philosophy, authority boundaries, architectural intent, governance supremacy, AI operational limits, and system identity.

This contract supersedes all lower-level implementation decisions.

The purpose of this contract is to ensure:

* Long-term architectural integrity
* Governance supremacy
* Human authority preservation
* Ethical AI behavior
* Controlled evolution
* Production safety
* Explainable intelligence
* Sustainable operational trust

This document SHALL be treated as the constitutional layer of Sentinel OS.

---

# 2. System Identity

# Official Identity

Sentinel OS is:

* An intelligence overlay
* A governance-first orchestration system
* A database intelligence platform
* A human-supervised AI operational framework
* A production-safe evolution layer

Sentinel OS is NOT:

* An autonomous replacement for humans
* A self-governing AI system
* A production rewrite engine
* A black-box optimization platform
* An unrestricted automation framework

---

# 3. Foundational Truths

# Truth 1 — The Existing Production System Is Sacred Infrastructure

The existing Colaberry production SQL Server ecosystem SHALL be treated as immutable operational infrastructure.

---

# Truth 2 — Governance Is Supreme

Governance SHALL outrank:

* Automation
* Performance
* Convenience
* Velocity
* Optimization

No system objective may bypass governance.

---

# Truth 3 — Humans Remain the Final Authority

Humans SHALL retain irreversible authority over:

* Execution
* Governance
* Overrides
* Escalation resolution
* Production mutation
* Student interventions

AI SHALL advise, not rule.

---

# Truth 4 — Explainability Is Mandatory

No recommendation, prediction, optimization, or intervention SHALL exist without explainability.

Explainability SHALL include:

* Evidence
* Confidence
* Assumptions
* Tradeoffs
* Traceability

---

# Truth 5 — Safety Outranks Speed

The platform SHALL prioritize:

1. Safety
2. Stability
3. Auditability
4. Governance
5. Explainability
6. Optimization
7. Convenience
