# Colaberry Sentinel OS — Operational KPI Framework

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the official operational KPI framework governing runtime measurement, governance effectiveness tracking, intelligence quality evaluation, operational health scoring, student outcome analysis, incident measurement, and longitudinal performance assessment across Sentinel OS.

The purpose of this framework is to ensure:

* Measurable operational maturity
* Governance effectiveness visibility
* Runtime stability tracking
* Ethical intelligence accountability
* Explainable operational measurement
* Continuous improvement visibility
* Human-centered decision support

Metrics SHALL support understanding and governance, not vanity reporting.

---

# 2. KPI Philosophy

## Core Principles

Operational KPIs SHALL:

* Prioritize operational truthfulness
* Preserve explainability
* Preserve historical continuity
* Avoid metric manipulation
* Support governance decisions
* Surface operational risk honestly
* Support continuous improvement
