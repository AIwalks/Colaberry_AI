# Colaberry Sentinel OS — Historical Retention Policy

# 1. Purpose

This document defines the official historical retention policy governing telemetry retention, audit preservation, governance history, execution lineage, incident records, student intelligence history, and long-term operational reconstruction across Sentinel OS.

The purpose of this policy is to ensure:

* Long-term operational traceability
* Governance accountability
* Historical replay capability
* Explainable longitudinal intelligence
* Compliance-aware persistence
* Sustainable storage governance
* Ethical historical preservation

Historical retention SHALL prioritize governance integrity, forensic reconstructability, and operational trust over short-term storage optimization.

---

# 2. Retention Philosophy

## Core Principles

Retention SHALL:

* Preserve governance lineage
* Preserve auditability
* Preserve replayability
* Preserve explainability
* Preserve operational trust
* Support forensic investigation
* Avoid hidden historical mutation
